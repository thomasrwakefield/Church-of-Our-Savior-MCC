Our Mission

Following Jesus — His Way, for His Glory

We exist to follow Jesus with sincerity, love people like He did, and make disciples who walk with Him in truth, humility, and grace. Every gathering is an invitation to open the Scriptures, pray, and let the Holy Spirit lead our steps — not programs or trends.

Disciples over Consumers

We measure faithfulness by obedience, repentance, fruit, and love — never by numbers or hype.

Spirit-Led Ministry

Methods come and go; Jesus leads forever. We depend on God’s Word and His Spirit for direction.

  • 1

    Sunday Hospitality

    Arrive early to welcome families, guide guests, and prepare the sanctuary for worship.

  • 2

    Food Distribution Team

    Serve our Sunday outreach at 12:30 PM by packing boxes, praying with families, and encouraging neighbors.

  • 3

    Encourage people through prayer, visits, and notes throughout the week, especially after Thursday Bible Study at 6:00 PM.

Leadership

A Shepherding Heart for Our Church

“My heart is not to impress you with a church building or a religious routine, but to invite you into a real, growing walk with Jesus Christ.” — Pastor Tommy Wakefield

At Lake LA Baptist Church, leadership is measured by Christ-likeness, biblical qualification, and a Spirit-led life. We follow those who follow Jesus in humility, holiness, and love.

About Us

Our Story Is About God’s Faithfulness

We gather to worship, open the Bible, pray, and serve our neighbors — not to build a name for ourselves, but to make much of Jesus.

Christ-Centered

Everything we are flows from who Jesus is and what He has done.

Bible-Practicing

We aim to hear God’s Word and do it — together, every day.

People-Loving

We welcome, disciple, and send people to love others as Christ has loved us.

Our history is a testimony of God forming a people by His Word for His glory and the good of our community. We continue to trust Jesus to build His church as we follow Him with humility, hold fast to Scripture, and love those He places in our path.
