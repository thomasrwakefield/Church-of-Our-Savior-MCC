All Are Welcome

An Open and Affirming Spiritual Home

Church of Our Savior MCC is an open and affirming Christian church in Boynton Beach, Florida, welcoming LGBTQ+ individuals, families, friends, and allies to experience God’s unconditional love.

We are rooted in Christ, centered on Scripture, and committed to creating a safe, joyful space where every person can worship authentically and grow in faith.

Inclusive Community

We celebrate the diverse stories God brings together and affirm the sacred worth of every person.

Christ-Centered Worship

Our services blend heartfelt prayer, uplifting music, and practical teaching grounded in Jesus’ love.
