Community Care

Sharing Hope Through Compassionate Outreach

Our inclusive food and care ministries reflect God’s unconditional love for Boynton Beach.

Weekly Food Outreach

Church of Our Savior MCC distributes fresh groceries and pantry staples to neighbors in need. Volunteers offer prayer, connection, and encouragement to every guest.

Schedule

  • Sunday: Food outreach begins after worship.
  • Location: Church of Our Savior MCC courtyard.
  • Participation: All are welcome to receive or volunteer.

How to Volunteer

Join our inclusive volunteer team to pack groceries, offer hospitality, or deliver food to homebound neighbors.
