Community Care

Food Ministry & Fellowship

Every Sunday at 12:30 PM—right after worship—we share fresh food boxes, pray with families, and point hearts to Christ.

Why We Serve

Jesus fed multitudes and taught His disciples to care for the hungry in His name. Our food ministry is a simple way to love our neighbors and open doors for the gospel. Each box is accompanied by prayer, Scripture, and a kind conversation.

Sunday Schedule

  • 11:00 AM — Sunday Worship
  • 12:10 PM — Volunteers gather for prayer & setup
  • 12:30 PM — Food Distribution begins

How You Can Help

  • Arrive by 12:10 PM to load boxes, stage produce, and pray with the team.
  • Offer a friendly smile, help carry food to cars, and check on each household’s needs.
  • Follow up during the week with a phone call or visit as the Lord leads.
Email to Volunteer

Need Food?

No sign-up is required. Come after Sunday worship at 11:00 AM or arrive by 12:30 PM. Boxes are available while supplies last, and we are glad to pray with you.

Scripture That Shapes This Ministry

“For I was an hungred, and ye gave me meat: I was thirsty, and ye gave me drink: I was a stranger, and ye took me in.” — Matthew 25:35

We serve because Jesus first served us. We feed because He is the Bread of Life. We pray because only He can change hearts.
