Plan Your Visit

Welcome to “The Best Little Church in Boynton Beach”

Church of Our Savior MCC is ready to greet you with open arms, inclusive worship, and God’s unconditional love.

When & Where

Join Us This Sunday

  • Primary Service: Sunday Worship at 10:00 AM (in-person & Facebook Live)
  • Location: Church of Our Savior MCC, 2011 S Federal Hwy, Boynton Beach, FL 33435
  • Parking: On-site parking and accessible entry are available.

Arrive a few minutes early to meet our hospitality team, enjoy coffee, and settle into the sanctuary. We can’t wait to meet you!

Watch Live on Facebook Get Directions

What to Expect

  • Warm, affirming welcome for LGBTQ+ people, families, and allies.
  • Christ-centered worship with music, prayer, and Communion.
  • Casual, come-as-you-are atmosphere. No dress code required.
  • Opportunities to connect with pastors and members after worship.

Families & Kids

Children and youth are welcome in worship. Activity bags and family seating are available.

Accessibility

Our campus offers accessible seating and restrooms. Let us know if you need ASL interpretation or other accommodations.

After Worship

Stay for fellowship, refreshments, and introductions to ministries that match your interests.

Contact Us

Have questions before you arrive? Send us a note and we’ll be glad to help you plan your visit.

Prefer email? Reach us at info@churchofoursaviormcc.org.
