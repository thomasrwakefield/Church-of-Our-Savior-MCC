Kids & Families

Children’s classes are offered during both Sunday School (10:00 AM) and Sunday Worship (11:00 AM). We value Scripture, prayer, and a safe, encouraging environment for every child.

Parking

Parking is available on-site near the church building. If you need assistance when you arrive, let us know—we’ll be glad to help.

What to Expect

  • Warm welcome and simple, sincere worship
  • Public reading and preaching of Scripture
  • Prayer, encouragement, and fellowship
  • Come as you are—no dress code required

Community Care

Food Distribution — Sundays at 12:30 PM

After our Sunday worship service we distribute food to anyone in need. You’re welcome to receive a box for yourself or a friend—or serve alongside us.
