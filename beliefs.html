Stained Glass Stories

Light That Welcomes Everyone

Each window at Church of Our Savior MCC radiates hope, justice, and the inclusive love of Christ.

Our Windows, Our Witness

The stained glass windows of Church of Our Savior MCC shine with stories of faith, resilience, and LGBTQ+ pride. Sunlight pours through every color, reminding us that God’s love embraces the entire spectrum of humanity.

As an inclusive church in Boynton Beach, we cherish these windows as sacred art and as a proclamation that everyone belongs in God’s house.

Rainbow Promise

A cascade of rainbow hues celebrates God’s covenant and affirms LGBTQ+ identities as gifts to the church.

We gather beneath this light to renew our promise: no one walks alone.

Spirit of Justice

Flames of the Holy Spirit move through this window, reminding us to advocate for justice, safety, and dignity for every neighbor.

It inspires our prayers for a world where love wins.

Community of Welcome

Figures gathered around the Table remind us that Communion is for everyone, no exceptions.

We open our doors and hearts to neighbors seeking a safe spiritual home.

Peaceful Presence

A dove descending over waves symbolizes baptismal grace and the Spirit’s comfort for all who are weary.

We pray under this window for healing, reconciliation, and peace.

Preserving Sacred Beauty

We lovingly maintain these stained glass windows so they will continue to inspire future generations. Your gifts help restore frames, protect glass, and share their stories with the wider community.
