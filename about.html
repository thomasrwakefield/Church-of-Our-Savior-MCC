About Us

Welcoming All Into God’s Embrace

Church of Our Savior MCC is an inclusive Christian community sharing the hope of Jesus in Boynton Beach and beyond.

Our Story

Rooted in the Metropolitan Community Churches movement, Church of Our Savior MCC has long been a refuge where LGBTQ+ people, families, and allies encounter God’s unconditional love. We began as a small circle of believers longing for an affirming, Christ-centered community in Boynton Beach. Today, we continue to grow as a joyful congregation committed to inclusion, justice, and compassion.

Every worship gathering, pastoral visit, and outreach project proclaims the simple gospel that God’s love is for everyone. We celebrate Holy Communion weekly, sing songs of hope, and share testimonies of God’s faithfulness in everyday life.

Open & Affirming

We affirm LGBTQ+ people, couples, and families as beloved children of God.

Christ-Centered

Jesus is at the heart of our worship, preaching, and service in Boynton Beach.

Community-Minded

We serve neighbors with compassion, hospitality, and practical support.

What MCC Means to Us

Metropolitan Community Churches (MCC) is a global fellowship founded in 1968 to proclaim God’s inclusive love, especially for LGBTQ+ people who have been marginalized by religious communities. As part of MCC, we join churches around the world that boldly celebrate sacraments, advocate for justice, and offer pastoral care without exception.

Our MCC affiliation shapes our theology and mission: we believe God’s grace is radically inclusive, we honor diverse gender identities and expressions, and we invite everyone to fully participate in ministry and leadership.

We Live This Out By

  • Practicing open Communion and celebrating every person’s sacred worth.
  • Centering worship, prayer, and preaching on Jesus’ liberating love.
  • Standing with our neighbors for equality, safety, and belonging.
