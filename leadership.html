Pastoral Leadership

Our pastor proclaims the inclusive Gospel, provides pastoral care, and leads our congregation in service to Boynton Beach and beyond.

Our pastor embodies MCC’s global mission: to proclaim God’s inclusive love, especially for LGBTQ+ people who have been marginalized. Every sermon, pastoral visit, and community partnership is rooted in justice, hope, and the teachings of Jesus.

We believe leadership is shared. Staff and lay leaders partner with the pastor to foster worship, pastoral care, and outreach ministries that honor the sacred worth of every person.

If you would like to schedule a conversation or request pastoral care, please reach out.
