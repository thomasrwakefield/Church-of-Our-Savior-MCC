Worship & Ministries

Growing Together in God’s Unconditional Love

Experience inclusive worship, vibrant ministries, and compassionate outreach designed to welcome everyone in Boynton Beach.

Sunday Worship

We gather each Sunday at 10:00 AM for an uplifting service that blends MCC tradition with contemporary expression. Our worship is prayerful, justice-oriented, and celebratory of LGBTQ+ people and allies.

What to Expect

  • Warm greetings from ushers ready to answer questions.
  • Inclusive liturgy, music, and prayer centered on Jesus.
  • Weekly Communion open to everyone.
  • Hybrid worship for in-person attendees and Facebook Live participants.

Accessibility

  • Accessible entrance and seating options.
  • Hospitality volunteers ready to assist every guest.
  • Printed worship guides and online bulletin links.

Ministries of Care & Connection

We cultivate ministries that reflect Jesus’ heart for healing, justice, and belonging. Explore ways to grow in faith and serve alongside others.

Spiritual Growth

Join seasonal Bible studies, MCC educational resources, and prayer gatherings that embrace authentic questions.

Inclusive Fellowship

Experience small groups and social gatherings that celebrate our diverse community.

Pastoral Care

Receive compassionate support through visits, prayer partners, and counseling referrals.

Community Outreach

Serve Boynton Beach through food assistance, advocacy, and neighborhood partnerships that extend Christ’s welcome.

Get Involved

We would love to help you find a ministry fit. Whether you love hospitality, music, teaching, or justice work, there is a place for you.

Music & Worship Arts

Our music team blends contemporary songs and MCC favorites to tell the story of God’s love. Vocalists, instrumentalists, readers, and artists all have a home here.

“Sing to the Lord a new song, for God has done marvelous things!” — Psalm 98:1

If you feel called to share your gifts, we will support you with rehearsals, mentorship, and a loving team.
